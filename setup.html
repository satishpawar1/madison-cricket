🏏 MCC Stats Chat

Get AI-powered cricket stats on your phone — for free

1

Register your Gmail

We need your Gmail to share the MCC stats sheet with you.

👉 Register your Gmail here
2

Create a free Claude account

Go to claude.ai → Sign up with Google.
Free, no credit card needed.

3

Connect Google Drive

In Claude: Settings → Integrations → Google Drive → Connect
Sign in with the same Google account you registered above.

4

Create a dedicated MCC project

  • Click Projects in the left sidebar
  • Click New Project
  • Name it: MCC Cricket Stats
5

Add the stats sheet to the project

  • Inside the project, click + or Add Content
  • Select Google Drive
  • Search MCC and select the shared sheet
  • It stays connected — no need to re-add each time
6

Start chatting!

Open your MCC Cricket Stats project → New Conversation → ask away.

Things you can ask

  • "Who are the top run scorers this season?"
  • "How has [player name] been bowling lately?"
  • "Which bowler has the best economy?"
  • "Show our last 5 match results"
  • "Who has the most wickets?"
Note: If Google Drive doesn't appear as an option inside the project, you may need a Claude Pro account ($20/month). The free tier supports up to 5 projects but Drive integration inside projects may require Pro.
